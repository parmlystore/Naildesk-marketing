常见问题解答

Can two clients book the same time slot?两位顾客可以预约同一时段吗？+
No — this is protected at the database level. Even if two people click confirm at the exact same second, only the first succeeds.不可以——系统在数据库层面进行了保护。即使两人同时点击确认，也只有最先点击的一方能成功预约。
Do I need Stripe for deposits?收取定金需要用到 Stripe 吗？+
No. You set your own deposit amount and bank details — clients transfer directly to you, no fees taken.不需要。您可以自行设置定金金额与银行账户信息，顾客直接转账给您，无需支付任何手续费。
What if I need help with ABN, GST, or bookkeeping?如果我需要 ABN、GST 或记账方面的帮助怎么办？+
Our in-app Support page can connect you with a registered professional for these services.系统内的支持页面可以为您对接注册专业人士，协助处理这些事务。
Can clients cancel or reschedule themselves?顾客可以自行取消或改期吗？+
Yes — using their booking reference and email, with no account or login needed.可以——顾客只需使用预约编号和邮箱即可操作，无需注册或登录账号。
